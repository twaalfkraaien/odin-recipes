Ingredients

Cheese

  • 1LB of Mozzarella
  • 1LB of Colby Jack
  • ½ of Sharp Cheddar
  • Shred all cheese, divide and set to the side.

Seasonings

  • 2tsp Salt
  • 1tsp Pepper
  • 1tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1tsp Smoked Paprika

Other Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • A Can of Evaporated Milk
  • Heavy Cream
  • Dijon Mustard

Steps

  1. Boil your pasta about halfway. Check it Periodically, if its slightly hard cook it longer. If its really soft, you've cooked it too long.
  2. Preheat your oven to 350⁰
  3. Make a Roux
    1. Take 3 Tablespoons of butter and melt it into a pot on low/medium heat.
    2. Once the butter is melted, add half of your Seasonings.
    3. Mix that together, then add 3tps of flour.
    4. Once all is mixed together, add your evaporated milk and start whisking so there aren't any clumps.
    5. As it thickens add 2 cups of heavy cream, whisk well, then add the rest of the seasonings.
    6. Add a tablespoon of mustard/ground mustard, mix until it's around a tomato soup thickness.
    7. Start adding half of the cheese, wait until the cheese melts to add more. Continue whisking as you add cheese.
  4. Begin mixing the pasta with the roux, get all of the pasta coated.
  5. Get your baking dish ready, cooking spray and all.
  6. Layer the dish with the Macaroni, cheese you set aside, and repeat until you're left with only cheese on the top.
  7. Put it into the oven for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Broil it after for 2 Minutes maximum for a nice golden top.
