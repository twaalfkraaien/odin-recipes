Ingredients

  • A stick of butter (about half a cup)
  • ½ cup of white sugar
  • ¾ cup of brown sugar
  • A teaspoon of vanilla
  • One egg
  • A pinch of baking powder
  • 1 and ¼ cup of flour

Steps

  1. Room temp butter, cream it with the sugar until combined into wet sand texture
  2. Add your vanilla and egg and mix until well combined again and the mixture falls off the whisk in ribbons
  3. Add your baking powder and flour and mix again. (Don't worry if it seems too wet.)
  4. Add in your chocolate chips or chunks or both
  5. Mix again and let the dough rest for at least 30 minutes. It'll firm up into a rollable dough
  6. Scoop 12 cookies with your hand or an ice cream scoop and put them on a tray with greased parchment paper
  7. Bake at 350⁰F for 13 minutes and that's it
